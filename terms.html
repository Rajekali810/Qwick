Welcome to Qwicklumen Institute for Engineers. By accessing or using our services, you agree to comply with the following terms and conditions:

1. Service Overview

We act as a bridge between job seekers and companies. We do not hire directly but connect you to potential employers. The responsibility for hiring lies with the companies.

2. Fees Structure

Our fee is structured as follows:

  • 50% of the total fee is charged upon application.
  • The remaining 50% is due upon successful placement in a job.
  • If you are not selected by a company, we offer you another opportunity with a different company.
  • If you choose not to continue, we will refund the entire fee.

We pride ourselves on our integrity. We do not engage in deceptive practices. Our commitment is to provide a 100% trustworthy service. You can trust us to assist you in your job search!
